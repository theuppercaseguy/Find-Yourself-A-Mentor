Finding a Mentor is our 4th semester project for the course of DBMS. This website is aimed at closing the mentoring gap and drive equity through quality mentoring for students. We are connecting and fueling opportunities for students at campus. Solve your problems with leading mentors at your side! We our on a mission to help students maximize their academic success and fulfillment. Become a mentor and get a chance to gain a certificate from your respective institute, provided by Finding a Mentor. It will serve as a platform to connect students and professionals around the campus and create an ecosystem of opportunities.
